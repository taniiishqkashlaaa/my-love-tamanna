1. Project Overview

Catch The Hearts is a simple arcade-style browser game built using Next.js.
The player controls a character positioned at the bottom of the screen and must catch falling hearts.

A total of 10 hearts will fall from the top one by one.
Each heart falls straight downward in a linear path, but the horizontal starting position changes randomly for every heart.

The goal is to catch as many hearts as possible before all 10 have fallen.

2. Core Gameplay Concept
Objective

The player must move left and right to align underneath falling hearts and catch them before they hit the bottom boundary.

Game Duration

Exactly 10 hearts fall per game session.

After the 10th heart finishes falling, the game ends automatically.

Scoring

Score starts at 0

Each caught heart increases score by 1

Final score is displayed as:

Score: X / 10
